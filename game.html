How to play

What is recyclable and what is not? Click on the start button to test your knowledge. Use the WASD keys on your keyboard to move the Earth Dude and pick up the item that appears on the screen. Your task is to determine what item goes in which bin and move Earth Dude to the correct bin. There is a recycling bin on the bottom-left and a trash-bin on the bottom right. So if you pick up an aluminum can, you should move Earth Dude to the recycling bin You get a point for adding an item to the correct bin and you lose a point for adding it to the incorrect bin. Get the highest score before within 60 Seconds or press the stop button to end the game.
