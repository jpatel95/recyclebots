Who are we?

We are RecycleBots, Inc. - a not for profit organization started by a group of environmentally-aware students, to organize recycling projects on campuses.


What do we do?

RecycleBots maintains specialized recycling stations to collect electronic waste on campuses. Here you can find information and links to various aspects of recycling and its advantages. We also hope to provide the means to introduce and educate young people in the concepts of recycling and its benefits to the planet we live in.

If you're interested in making a difference at your school, workplace, or community, check out our store to find a product suitable for your needs!

Looking for innovative and creative ideas on how to be smart about your waste and how to reduce your carbon footprint? Take a look at the thriving forum where environment-conscious individuals come to discuss related topics.

You can also find our game page to pump you up about saving the Earth! After that, go check out the campus map to start your future of smart recylcing choices!


What Can You Do?

The first step in being a responsible inhabitant of Earth is to reduce waste going to landfills. Learn about what you can recycle and how to recycle them. Here's a comprehensive list of recyclable materials:

  • Metal
  • Steel Cans
  • Aluminum Can
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Bakeware
  • Metal
  • Paper
  • Plastic
  • Glass

You can find more details about these materials here.

The following items are not recycleable and need to be disposed of properly:

  • Batteries
  • Milk Carton
  • Hazardous Waste
  • Motor Oil

Ask your local recylcing facility if they recycle all these materials. If not, your local directory should have listings for facilities that specialize in a particular recyclable.
